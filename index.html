What we offer

The financial
leadership your
organization needs

Headwaters North works with nonprofits, small businesses, and growing organizations that need senior financial oversight — but aren't ready for, or don't need, a full-time hire.

Fractional CFO

Senior financial leadership on a part-time basis. Cash flow strategy, board reporting, fundraising support, and long-range financial planning.

Controller Services

Month-end close, reconciliations, chart of accounts management, and audit-ready financial statements. The operational core, handled with precision.

Budgeting & Forecasting

Annual budgets, rolling forecasts, and variance analysis that help leadership understand where they are — and where they're going.

Grant & Fund Accounting

Nonprofit-specific financial management including restricted fund tracking, grant reporting, and compliance-ready records for auditors and funders.
